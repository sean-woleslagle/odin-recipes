Lasagna

Home

Description:

This is one of the most delicious meals in the world.

Ladies and gentlemen, lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Flat Pasta
  • Red Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Beef
  • Italian Seasonings
  1. First bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta to water, cook to al dente.
  3. Make 1 layer of pasta in 9x5 baking tray.
  4. Add a layer of tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, mozarella cheese, beef, and Italian seasonings.
  5. Add another layer of pasta.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you reach the top of the baking dish.
  7. Place in oven for 45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
  8. Divide the pasta evenly, serve, and enjoy!
