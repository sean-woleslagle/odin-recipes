Description:

This is one of the most delicious meals in the world.

Ladies and gentlemen, sushi!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • fish
  • vegetables
  • Nori (seaweed wrap)
  1. First cook a lot of rice in a rice cooker.
  2. Wet Nori and place on a bamboo sushi roller.
  3. Layer the rice on top of the Nori.
  4. Add the fish of your choice, in a line, in the middle of the rice.
  5. Add the veggie of your choice, in a line next to the fish.
  6. Tightly roll the ingredients together using the bamboo roller.
  7. Cut the roll using a sharp knife, serve, and enjoy!
