Description:

This is one of the most delicious meals in the world.

Ladies and gentlemen, pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough - (00 bread flour)
  • Red Tomato Sauce
  • Peperroni
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Italian Seasonings
  1. First knead your pizza dough.
  2. Toss your pizza dough to make a disk, like a frisbee.
  3. Add 1 layer of your tomato sauce.
  4. Add a layer of cheese.
  5. Add a final layer of peperroni.
  6. Bake in an oven for 14 minutes at 500 degrees.
  7. Carefully remove from oven, let sit for a few minutes.
  8. Cut the pizza evenly, serve, and enjoy!
